Kernel Season's White Cheddar
A white cheddar topping salt. I normally like cheesy popcorn, but this salt was really not for me. I also had my boyfriend and roommates try it and they did not like it either.

Salty

Flavocal
Most similar to movie theatre popcorn salt. In my opinion, this is the best salt on the market.
Amish Country's Original Ballpark Style Popcorn Salt
As the title suggests, this salt is similar to that made at sporting events. At my apartment this is a favorite among me and my roommates for salty flavor.
Kernel Season's Movie Theater Butter Salt
Another movie theater inspired topping salt. This salt is very similar to flavocal, but misses the mark by a bit. Definetly still a great option, but just not the best.
