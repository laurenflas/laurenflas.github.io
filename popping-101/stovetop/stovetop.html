Stovetop

Best For

Stovetop popcorn is the best method if you would like to flavor your popcorn. Because you pop the popcorn in oil you can also include flavor salts into the cooking to get the kernels to absorb the salt flavor.
You cannot cook stovetop popcorn on an induction stove, as you would just scratch up the top of it.

Tips

I always like to put in more oil than might be needed. It can be a real pain if your popcorn burns to the bottom of the pan. When popping you'll also want to make sure youre shaking the pot back and forth to keep the unpopped kernels at the bottom of the pot and from staying in one spot and burning in the pan.
