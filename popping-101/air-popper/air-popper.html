Air Popper

Best For

Air popping is best for those who want to eat popcorn as a healthier snack. Because there is no oil involved with popping the popcorn, is the healthiest way to consume popcorn.

Tips

When popping with an air popper, you'll want to make sure you have a large bowl underneath the spout. Popcorn tends to fly everywhere so you'll want to be able to catch it! Additionally, if you want butter on your popcorn you'll have to have a big bowl to be able to shake it in properly.
Because the popcorn isn't popped with oil, topping salts don't stick very well to air popped popcorn. If you do want to use topping salts on air popped popcorn, I recommend getting an oil mister that you can use to spray oil or butter onto the popcorn to hold the salt.
