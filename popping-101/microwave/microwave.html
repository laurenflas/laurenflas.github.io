Microwave

Best For

Microwave popcorn is a very convenient and low effort way to pop popcorn.

Tips

The most important thing when cooking microwave popcorn is to listen to it in the microwave. Never blindly trust the time given on the packaging for how long it should be in the microwave. You'll want to pull it out when theres 4-5 seconds between pops to avoid it burning.
